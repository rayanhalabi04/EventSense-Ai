Elegant Weddings Pricing and Packages

Silver Package: 8,000 USD, covers up to 100 guests, includes venue hire for six hours, in-house catering, and basic floral styling.
Gold Package: 14,000 USD, covers up to 180 guests, adds a plated three-course dinner, premium floral styling, and a dedicated coordinator.
Platinum Package: 22,000 USD, covers up to 300 guests, adds live entertainment, a tasting session, and full-day venue access.

Peak-season weddings in June and September carry a 15 percent surcharge on the package fee.
