Elegant Weddings Cancellation Policy

Couples may cancel within seven calendar days of signing and receive a full deposit refund. After the first seven days the deposit becomes non-refundable because the date is removed from public availability.

Cancellation requests between fifty-nine and thirty days before the wedding receive a 40 percent refund of paid package fees, excluding the deposit. Cancellations inside thirty days are not refundable.

One date transfer is allowed with no transfer fee when requested at least ninety days before the original wedding date.
