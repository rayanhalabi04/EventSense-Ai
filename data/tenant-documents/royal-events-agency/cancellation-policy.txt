Royal Events Cancellation Policy

Clients may cancel within fourteen calendar days of signing and receive a 50 percent refund of the deposit. After fourteen days the deposit is non-refundable.

Cancellation requests between forty-five and twenty days before the event receive a 30 percent refund of paid package fees. Cancellations inside twenty days are not refundable.

A date transfer is allowed for a 500 USD transfer fee when requested at least sixty days before the original event date.
