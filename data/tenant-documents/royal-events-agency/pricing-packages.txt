Royal Events Pricing and Packages

Classic Package: 10,000 USD, covers up to 120 guests, includes venue hire, standard decor, and buffet catering.
Royal Package: 18,000 USD, covers up to 250 guests, adds a plated dinner, luxury decor, and an event manager.
Imperial Package: 30,000 USD, covers up to 400 guests, adds live entertainment, premium bar service, and full-venue exclusivity.

Saturday events carry a 20 percent surcharge on the package fee.
