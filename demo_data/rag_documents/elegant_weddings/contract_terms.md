# Elegant Weddings — Contract Terms

*This is fictional sample contract language created for demonstration of EventSense
AI. It is not legal advice and does not represent a real agreement.*

## 1. Parties and Scope

This agreement is between Elegant Weddings ("the Agency") and the couple ("the
Client"). It covers the planning and coordination services selected by the Client in
one of the Agency's packages: Premium, Gold, or Silver. The selected package and any
agreed add-ons are listed in the booking confirmation, which forms part of this
agreement.

## 2. Packages

2.1 The **Premium package includes decoration, catering coordination, and photography
coordination**, a dedicated lead planner, and on-the-day coordination.

2.2 The **Gold package includes planning support, basic decoration coordination, and
vendor coordination**, with on-the-day coordination.

2.3 The **Silver package includes consultation, timeline planning, and supplier
recommendations**. The Silver package is advisory and does not include on-the-day
coordination.

2.4 Services not expressly listed in the selected package are not included unless
added as a paid custom service and recorded in writing.

## 3. Booking and Deposit

3.1 A booking deposit is required to reserve the Client's date. **The deposit confirms
the wedding date only after payment is received and recorded.**

3.2 **The booking deposit is non-refundable after booking confirmation.**

## 4. Payment

4.1 **The final package balance is due 14 days before the event.**

4.2 Payment is considered received only when it appears and is recorded in the
Agency's booking system. Late payment of the final balance may pause planning and
affect time-sensitive vendor arrangements.

4.3 Add-on services requested after booking are billed separately and may require
payment before the service is scheduled.

## 5. Cancellation

5.1 **Cancellation requests after confirmation must be reviewed by a manager.** A
planner cannot finalize a cancellation alone.

5.2 The non-refundable deposit applies on cancellation. Where the final balance has
been paid, any return is determined by manager review after accounting for services
delivered and vendor costs committed.

5.3 Rescheduling may be available subject to the Agency's team availability and is
also subject to manager review.

## 6. Guest Count

6.1 **Guest count changes must be confirmed at least 10 days before the event.**

6.2 **Guest count increases after the deadline require manager approval and may affect
catering availability.** Decreases after the deadline may not reduce costs already
committed with vendors.

## 7. Decoration Changes

7.1 **Decoration sample changes must be requested at least 14 days before the event.**
Requests made after this window may not be possible to fulfill, depending on supplier
availability.

## 8. Transportation

8.1 **The Agency does not provide guest airport transportation unless it is added as a
custom service** and recorded in writing in this agreement or an addendum.

## 9. Custom and Special Requests

9.1 The Client may request services outside the standard packages and add-ons.
**Unsupported or custom requests should be escalated to staff review.** No custom
request is binding until confirmed in writing by the Agency.

9.2 Staff may not waive the terms of this agreement, including the non-refundable
deposit. Any exception requires manager approval recorded in writing.

## 10. Vendor and Third-Party Costs

10.1 Vendor arrangements made on the Client's behalf may carry their own terms. Where
third-party costs are committed, those costs are the Client's responsibility.

## 11. Liability

11.1 The Agency provides planning and coordination services with reasonable care and
skill. The Agency is not responsible for the independent acts or omissions of
third-party vendors selected or approved by the Client.

## 12. Entire Agreement

12.1 This agreement, together with the booking confirmation and any written addenda,
forms the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes prior discussions.

## Summary of Key Terms

- Deposit reserves the date only after it is received and recorded.
- Deposit is non-refundable after booking confirmation.
- Final balance is due 14 days before the event.
- Guest count changes confirmed at least 10 days before the event; late increases
  need manager approval.
- Decoration sample changes requested at least 14 days before the event.
- Guest airport transportation only as a custom service.
- Cancellations and exceptions require manager review.
