# Elegant Weddings — Deposit & Payment Policy

*This is fictional sample policy text created for demonstration of EventSense AI.*

## Purpose

This policy explains how booking deposits and final balances work at Elegant
Weddings. It is written so that couples and our staff share the same understanding
of when a date is confirmed, what is refundable, and when payments are due.

## Booking Deposit

To begin planning, couples pay a booking deposit. The deposit reserves our planning
team for your chosen date and starts the formal planning process.

**The deposit confirms the wedding date only after payment is received and
recorded.** A date is not held simply because a couple has expressed interest or
verbally agreed. Until the deposit payment has been received and recorded in our
booking system, the date remains open and may be offered to other couples.

Once we confirm receipt of the deposit, we mark the booking as confirmed and send a
written booking confirmation that lists the date, the selected package, and the
balance due date.

## Deposit Refundability

**The booking deposit is non-refundable after booking confirmation.** Because the
deposit immediately reserves our planning team and removes your date from
availability, it cannot be refunded once the booking is confirmed.

If a couple decides not to proceed before the booking is confirmed — that is, before
the deposit has been received and recorded — no deposit has been taken and there is
nothing to refund.

## Final Balance

The remaining cost of your package is the final balance. **The final package balance
is due 14 days before the event.** We send a balance reminder ahead of the due date.

If the final balance is not paid by the due date, planning and coordination may be
paused until payment is received, and some time-sensitive vendor arrangements may be
affected. We strongly encourage couples to pay the balance on or before the due date
to keep all arrangements on schedule.

## Accepted Payment Methods

We accept bank transfer and card payment for both deposits and final balances. For
bank transfers, please include your booking reference so we can match the payment to
your booking quickly. A payment is considered received only once it appears and is
recorded in our booking system.

## Add-On Payments

Custom services and add-ons requested after booking are billed separately. Where an
add-on is requested close to the event date, payment may be required before the
service is scheduled. Add-ons do not change the deposit terms above.

## Changes and Cancellations

This document covers deposits and payments only. Requests to cancel a confirmed
booking are governed by our Cancellation Policy. In summary, **cancellation requests
after confirmation must be reviewed by a manager**, and the non-refundable nature of
the confirmed deposit still applies.

## Disputes and Special Cases

If a couple believes their situation is exceptional, they may ask our staff to review
it. Staff cannot override the non-refundable deposit rule on their own. **Unsupported
or custom requests should be escalated to staff review**, and any exception must be
approved by a manager and confirmed in writing.

## Summary of Key Dates and Rules

- Deposit reserves the date only after it is received and recorded.
- Deposit is non-refundable after booking confirmation.
- Final balance is due 14 days before the event.
- Cancellations after confirmation require manager review.
