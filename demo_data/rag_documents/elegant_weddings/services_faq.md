# Elegant Weddings — Services FAQ

*This is a fictional sample FAQ created for demonstration of EventSense AI.*

## General

**Who is Elegant Weddings for?**
We are a premium but warm wedding planning agency focused on organized, classic
weddings. We work with couples who want a clear plan, dependable coordination, and a
calm experience on the day.

**What planning packages do you offer?**
We offer three packages: Premium, Gold, and Silver. The Premium package includes
decoration, catering coordination, and photography coordination. The Gold package
includes planning support, basic decoration coordination, and vendor coordination.
The Silver package includes consultation, timeline planning, and supplier
recommendations. Full details are in our Pricing & Packages document.

## Booking & Payment

**How do I reserve my date?**
You reserve your date with a booking deposit. The deposit confirms the wedding date
only after payment is received and recorded. After that, the date is held for you.

**Is the deposit refundable?**
The booking deposit is non-refundable after booking confirmation. Please see our
Deposit Policy for the full terms.

**When is the final balance due?**
The final package balance is due 14 days before the event.

## Decoration

**Can I change my decoration choices?**
Yes, within our planning timeline. **Decoration sample changes must be requested at
least 14 days before the event.** This gives our team and suppliers time to source
and prepare the updated items. Requests made after this window may not be possible to
fulfill.

**Do you set up the decoration on the day?**
Yes. Decoration setup is included in the Premium package and as basic decoration
coordination in the Gold package. The Silver package is advisory and does not include
on-the-day decoration setup.

## Guest Count

**When do I need to confirm my guest count?**
Guest count changes must be confirmed at least 10 days before the event. Increases
after the deadline require manager approval and may affect catering availability. See
our Guest Count Policy for details.

## Transportation

**Do you arrange transportation for guests?**
**The agency does not provide guest airport transportation unless it is added as a
custom service.** Standard packages do not include guest airport pickups or shuttles.
If you would like transportation arranged, ask us and we will provide a custom quote
and add it to your contract.

## Catering

**Do you handle catering?**
We coordinate catering rather than cook it ourselves. In the Premium package we
manage menu selection and service flow with your catering vendor. In the Gold package
we coordinate vendors including catering. Final quantities are based on your confirmed
guest count.

## Changes & Special Requests

**What if I have a request that isn't on your list?**
We love thoughtful, personal touches. Some requests fall outside our standard
packages and add-ons, such as unusual installations, large-scale custom services, or
non-standard logistics. **Unsupported or custom requests should be escalated to staff
review** so a planner can confirm whether it is possible, how long it will take, and
what it will cost before anything is promised.

**Can staff approve exceptions to your policies?**
No. Staff cannot waive policies such as the non-refundable deposit on their own.
Exceptions require manager approval and are confirmed in writing.

## On the Day

**Will someone coordinate on the wedding day?**
Yes, with the Premium and Gold packages. A coordinator manages the run-of-show,
keeps vendors on schedule, and handles small issues so you can enjoy your day. The
Silver package is advisory and does not include on-the-day coordination.

## Quick Reference

- Deposit: non-refundable after booking confirmation.
- Final balance: due 14 days before the event.
- Guest count: confirm changes at least 10 days before the event.
- Decoration sample changes: request at least 14 days before the event.
- Guest airport transportation: only as a custom service.
- Custom or unsupported requests: escalated to staff review.
