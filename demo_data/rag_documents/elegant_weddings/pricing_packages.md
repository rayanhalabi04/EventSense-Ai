# Elegant Weddings — Pricing & Packages

*All figures below are sample, fictional pricing created for demonstration of EventSense AI. They do not represent a real company.*

## About Our Packages

Elegant Weddings is a premium but warm wedding planning agency. We specialize in
organized, classic weddings where every detail is planned in advance and confirmed
in writing. We offer three planning packages — Premium, Gold, and Silver — so that
couples can choose the level of coordination that fits their celebration. Every
package is delivered by a dedicated lead planner and a written planning timeline.

## Premium Package

The Premium package is our most complete offering and is recommended for couples who
want a fully coordinated, hands-off experience.

The **Premium package includes decoration, catering coordination, and photography
coordination.** Our team manages decoration design and setup, coordinates the menu
and service flow with the catering vendor, and coordinates the photography schedule
so that key moments are captured. The Premium package also includes a dedicated lead
planner, two pre-event planning meetings, a detailed run-of-show timeline, and
on-the-day coordination from setup through send-off.

Indicative price: 18,000 currency units.

## Gold Package

The Gold package is designed for couples who want strong planning support while
keeping some tasks in their own hands.

The **Gold package includes planning support, basic decoration coordination, and
vendor coordination.** This means our planners help you build and maintain your
planning timeline, coordinate the core decoration elements, and act as the central
point of contact for your booked vendors. The Gold package includes one pre-event
planning meeting and on-the-day coordination during the ceremony and reception.

Indicative price: 11,000 currency units.

## Silver Package

The Silver package is our entry-level planning option for couples who mostly want
expert guidance and direction.

The **Silver package includes consultation, timeline planning, and supplier
recommendations.** You receive a structured consultation, a written planning
timeline you can follow yourself, and a curated list of recommended suppliers that
match your style and budget. The Silver package is advisory in nature and does not
include on-the-day coordination.

Indicative price: 5,500 currency units.

## What Is Not Included

Unless explicitly listed, packages do not include venue rental fees, vendor service
charges, taxes, or custom add-on services. Custom services — such as guest
transportation, additional decoration installations, or extended planning hours —
can be quoted separately. **The agency does not provide guest airport transportation
unless it is added as a custom service.**

## Add-On Services

Couples can extend any package with paid add-ons, including additional planning
meetings, enhanced decoration packages, rehearsal coordination, and custom
transportation arrangements. Add-ons must be agreed in writing and added to the
contract before the event.

## Payment Overview

A booking deposit is required to reserve your wedding date. The **final package
balance is due 14 days before the event.** Detailed deposit and balance terms are
described in the Deposit Policy document. Pricing quoted at booking is held for the
contracted services; new add-ons requested later are billed at current rates.

## How to Choose

If you are unsure which package fits, we recommend starting with a consultation.
Many couples begin with Silver for guidance, then upgrade to Gold or Premium as
planning progresses. Upgrades are possible at any time before the final balance is
due, subject to availability of our planning team for your date.

## Escalation of Special Requests

Some couples have requests that fall outside our standard packages and add-ons.
**Unsupported or custom requests should be escalated to staff review** so that a
planner can confirm feasibility, timelines, and pricing before anything is promised.
