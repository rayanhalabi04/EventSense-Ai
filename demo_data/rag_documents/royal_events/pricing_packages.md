# Royal Events Agency — Pricing & Packages

*All figures below are sample, fictional pricing created for demonstration of
EventSense AI. They do not represent a real company.*

## About Our Packages

Royal Events Agency is a luxury event agency specializing in high-end weddings. Our
signature strengths are dramatic lighting, striking bridal entrance setups, and
premium end-to-end coordination. We offer three packages — Luxury, Royal Signature,
and Classic — each delivered by a senior event producer and a production team.

## Luxury Package

The Luxury package is our flagship full-production experience for couples who want a
spectacular, fully managed event.

The **Luxury package includes decoration, catering, lighting, and bridal entrance
setup.** Our team designs and installs the decoration, manages catering end to end,
engineers the event lighting, and produces a custom bridal entrance moment. The
Luxury package includes a senior event producer, a full production crew, multiple
planning sessions, and complete on-site management from load-in to teardown.

Indicative price: 42,000 currency units.

## Royal Signature Package

The Royal Signature package is built around design and atmosphere, for couples who
want a strong creative theme and dramatic staging.

The **Royal Signature package includes premium theme design, stage lighting, catering
coordination, and bridal entrance planning.** We develop a bespoke theme and design
language, engineer stage and feature lighting, coordinate your catering vendor, and
plan a memorable bridal entrance. The Royal Signature package includes a senior
producer and on-site management for the key event hours.

Indicative price: 28,000 currency units.

## Classic Package

The Classic package is our streamlined coordination offering for couples who want
professional management without full production.

The **Classic package includes basic event coordination, vendor communication, and
timeline management.** We coordinate the overall event flow, serve as the central
contact for your vendors, and maintain a detailed timeline. The Classic package does
not include lighting production, custom theme design, or bridal entrance staging.

Indicative price: 12,000 currency units.

## Add-On Services

Any package can be extended with paid add-ons, including enhanced lighting rigs,
additional staging, extended production hours, and VIP guest services. **The agency
can arrange VIP guest airport transportation only as a paid add-on.** Add-ons must be
agreed in writing and added to the contract before the event.

## What Is Not Included

Unless explicitly listed in your selected package, the following are not included:
venue rental, vendor service charges, taxes, and custom add-on services. The Classic
package in particular does not include lighting production or bridal entrance setup;
those are features of the Royal Signature and Luxury packages.

## Payment Overview

A booking deposit is required to reserve your date. **The final package balance is due
10 days before the event.** Detailed deposit and refund terms — including the 30-day
refund window — are described in the Deposit Policy document. Note that **payment
confirmation can take up to 24 hours after bank transfer receipt**, so please allow
time when paying close to a deadline.

## How to Choose

Couples who want maximum impact and full production choose Luxury. Couples focused on
a distinctive design and dramatic staging choose Royal Signature. Couples who want
professional coordination at a controlled budget choose Classic. Upgrades between
packages are possible before the final balance is due, subject to production team
availability for your date.

## Special Requests and Concerns

We welcome ambitious, personalized requests and will tell you honestly what is
feasible for your date and venue. If a client raises a serious concern or complaint
about their booking, we take it seriously: **high-risk client complaints should be
escalated to a manager** so they can be reviewed and resolved quickly.
