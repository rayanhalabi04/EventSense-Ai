# Royal Events Agency — Deposit & Payment Policy

*This is fictional sample policy text created for demonstration of EventSense AI.*

## Purpose

This policy explains how booking deposits, refunds, and final balances work at Royal
Events Agency. Because our events involve significant production commitments —
lighting, staging, and entrance setups — our deposit terms are tied to how far in
advance a cancellation occurs.

## Booking Deposit

To reserve your date and begin production planning, couples pay a booking deposit.
The deposit secures your date in our production calendar and allows us to begin
reserving crew and equipment.

A date is held once the deposit is confirmed. Please note that **payment confirmation
can take up to 24 hours after bank transfer receipt.** During that window, the
payment is being matched and recorded; your date is fully secured once confirmation
is complete.

## Deposit Refundability

Our deposit refund terms depend on timing:

- **The deposit is partially refundable if cancellation happens more than 30 days
  before the event.** If you cancel with more than 30 days remaining before the event
  date, you receive a partial refund of the deposit, after deducting production
  planning and any committed costs incurred up to that point.

- **The deposit is non-refundable if cancellation happens within 30 days of the
  event.** Within the final 30 days, crew, equipment, and vendor commitments are
  typically locked in, so the deposit cannot be refunded.

The cancellation date is the date we receive the written cancellation request.

## Final Balance

**The final package balance is due 10 days before the event.** We send a balance
reminder ahead of the due date. Because **payment confirmation can take up to 24 hours
after bank transfer receipt**, we recommend paying at least one full day before the
balance due date so that confirmation completes on time.

If the final balance is not confirmed by the due date, production scheduling may be
paused and certain lighting, staging, and vendor arrangements may be affected.

## Accepted Payment Methods

We accept bank transfer and card payment. For bank transfers, include your booking
reference so we can match the payment. Remember that bank transfer confirmation may
take up to 24 hours to be recorded.

## Add-On Payments

Paid add-ons — such as enhanced lighting, additional staging, or VIP guest airport
transportation — are billed separately. Add-ons requested close to the event may
require payment before they are scheduled.

## Cancellations and Manager Review

This document covers deposits and payments. Broader cancellation handling is in our
Cancellation Policy. If a client is upset or raises a serious concern about a refund
or cancellation, **high-risk client complaints should be escalated to a manager** for
review and resolution.

## Summary of Key Rules

- Date is secured once the deposit is confirmed; bank transfer confirmation can take
  up to 24 hours.
- Deposit is partially refundable if cancellation is more than 30 days before the
  event.
- Deposit is non-refundable if cancellation is within 30 days of the event.
- Final balance is due 10 days before the event.
- Serious complaints are escalated to a manager.
