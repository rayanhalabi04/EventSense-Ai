# Royal Events Agency — Contract Terms

*This is fictional sample contract language created for demonstration of EventSense
AI. It is not legal advice and does not represent a real agreement.*

## 1. Parties and Scope

This agreement is between Royal Events Agency ("the Agency") and the client ("the
Client"). It covers the production and coordination services selected by the Client in
one of the Agency's packages: Luxury, Royal Signature, or Classic. The selected
package and any agreed add-ons are listed in the booking confirmation, which forms
part of this agreement.

## 2. Packages

2.1 The **Luxury package includes decoration, catering, lighting, and bridal entrance
setup**, with a senior producer and full on-site production management.

2.2 The **Royal Signature package includes premium theme design, stage lighting,
catering coordination, and bridal entrance planning**, with on-site management for the
key event hours.

2.3 The **Classic package includes basic event coordination, vendor communication,
and timeline management**. It does not include lighting production, theme design, or
bridal entrance staging.

2.4 Services not expressly listed in the selected package are not included unless
added as a paid add-on and recorded in writing.

## 3. Booking and Deposit

3.1 A booking deposit is required to reserve the Client's date and secure production
resources.

3.2 **Payment confirmation can take up to 24 hours after bank transfer receipt.** The
date is fully secured once the deposit is confirmed.

## 4. Refunds

4.1 **The deposit is partially refundable if cancellation happens more than 30 days
before the event**, after deducting production planning and committed costs.

4.2 **The deposit is non-refundable if cancellation happens within 30 days of the
event.**

4.3 The cancellation date is the date the Agency receives the Client's written
cancellation request.

## 5. Payment

5.1 **The final package balance is due 10 days before the event.**

5.2 Because bank transfer confirmation can take up to 24 hours, the Client should pay
in time for confirmation to complete before the due date. Late confirmation may pause
production scheduling.

5.3 Add-on services are billed separately and may require payment before scheduling.

## 6. Guest Count

6.1 **Guest count changes are allowed up to 7 days before the event.**

6.2 **Guest count increases after the 7-day deadline require catering and venue
approval** and may not be possible if quantities or layout are already locked.

## 7. Lighting and Production Changes

7.1 **Lighting setup changes must be confirmed at least 12 days before the event.**
Changes after this window may not be possible because lighting is engineered and
crewed in advance.

## 8. Transportation

8.1 **The Agency can arrange VIP guest airport transportation only as a paid add-on**,
recorded in writing in this agreement or an addendum. It is not included in any
standard package.

## 9. Complaints and Escalation

9.1 The Agency aims to resolve concerns quickly and fairly. **High-risk client
complaints should be escalated to a manager** for review and resolution.

9.2 Staff may not waive the terms of this agreement, including the timing-based
deposit rules. Any exception requires manager approval recorded in writing.

## 10. Vendor and Third-Party Costs

10.1 Lighting, staging, entrance, and catering resources are often reserved in
advance. Where these costs are committed, they are the Client's responsibility and are
factored into any refund.

## 11. Liability

11.1 The Agency provides production and coordination services with reasonable care and
skill. The Agency is not responsible for the independent acts or omissions of
third-party vendors selected or approved by the Client.

## 12. Entire Agreement

12.1 This agreement, together with the booking confirmation and any written addenda,
forms the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes prior discussions.

## Summary of Key Terms

- Deposit: partially refundable if cancelled more than 30 days before the event;
  non-refundable within 30 days.
- Bank transfer confirmation can take up to 24 hours.
- Final balance is due 10 days before the event.
- Guest count changes allowed up to 7 days before the event; late increases need
  catering and venue approval.
- Lighting setup changes confirmed at least 12 days before the event.
- VIP airport transportation is a paid add-on only.
- High-risk complaints are escalated to a manager.
