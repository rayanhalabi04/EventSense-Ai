# Royal Events Agency — Services FAQ

*This is a fictional sample FAQ created for demonstration of EventSense AI.*

## General

**Who is Royal Events Agency for?**
We are a luxury event agency for high-end weddings. We are known for dramatic
lighting, striking bridal entrance setups, and premium, hands-on coordination.

**What packages do you offer?**
We offer three packages: Luxury, Royal Signature, and Classic. The Luxury package
includes decoration, catering, lighting, and bridal entrance setup. The Royal
Signature package includes premium theme design, stage lighting, catering
coordination, and bridal entrance planning. The Classic package includes basic event
coordination, vendor communication, and timeline management. Full details are in our
Pricing & Packages document.

## Booking & Payment

**How do I secure my date?**
You secure your date with a booking deposit. Once the deposit is confirmed, your date
is held in our production calendar. Note that **payment confirmation can take up to 24
hours after bank transfer receipt.**

**Is the deposit refundable?**
It depends on timing. The deposit is partially refundable if cancellation happens
more than 30 days before the event, and non-refundable if cancellation happens within
30 days of the event. See our Deposit Policy for details.

**When is the final balance due?**
The final package balance is due 10 days before the event.

## Lighting & Production

**Can I change the lighting setup?**
Yes, within the production timeline. **Lighting setup changes must be confirmed at
least 12 days before the event.** Lighting is engineered and crewed in advance, so
changes after this window may not be possible.

**Which packages include lighting?**
Stage lighting is part of the Royal Signature package, and full event lighting is
part of the Luxury package. The Classic package does not include lighting production.

## Bridal Entrance

**Do you design the bridal entrance?**
Yes. Bridal entrance setup is included in the Luxury package, and bridal entrance
planning is included in the Royal Signature package. The Classic package does not
include entrance staging.

## Guest Count

**When do I confirm my guest count?**
Guest count changes are allowed up to 7 days before the event. Increases after the
7-day deadline require catering and venue approval. See our Guest Count Policy.

## Transportation

**Can you arrange airport transportation for VIP guests?**
**The agency can arrange VIP guest airport transportation only as a paid add-on.** It
is not included in any standard package. Tell us your VIP arrivals and we will quote
the service and add it to your contract.

## Complaints & Escalation

**What if I'm unhappy with something?**
We want to know right away. **High-risk client complaints should be escalated to a
manager** so they can review the situation and resolve it quickly. Examples include a
client who is highly dissatisfied, threatens a payment dispute, or raises a serious
service concern.

**Can staff override your policies?**
No. Staff cannot waive timing-based deposit rules or other terms on their own.
Exceptions require manager approval recorded in writing.

## Quick Reference

- Deposit: partially refundable if cancelled more than 30 days before the event;
  non-refundable within 30 days.
- Final balance: due 10 days before the event.
- Bank transfer confirmation: can take up to 24 hours.
- Guest count: changes allowed up to 7 days before the event.
- Lighting setup changes: confirm at least 12 days before the event.
- VIP airport transportation: paid add-on only.
- High-risk complaints: escalated to a manager.
