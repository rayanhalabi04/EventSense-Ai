Royal Events Agency Pricing Packages

The Silver City Package starts at 6,300 USD for up to 150 guests. It includes venue sourcing, vendor shortlist support, timeline planning, and one on-site coordinator.

The Orchid Dome Package starts at 9,900 USD for up to 220 guests. It includes a mirrored aisle, ceiling florals, lighting design, premium guest welcome signage, and two coordinators.

The Crown Production Package starts at 16,800 USD for multi-day celebrations. It includes creative direction, hospitality desk planning, VIP transport coordination, and production schedule management.

Royal Events Agency requires a 35 percent planning retainer to begin work. Venue rental and catering are billed separately by the selected vendors.
