Royal Events Agency FAQ

Royal Events can coordinate hotel room blocks, VIP airport pickups, welcome gifts, and private security when those services are included in the planning scope.

Final production timelines are delivered ten business days before the event. Vendor call sheets are shared after the client approves the final timeline.

Fireworks, drone shows, and rooftop installations require venue approval, municipal permits, and vendor insurance certificates before they can be confirmed.

The Orchid Dome design uses ceiling florals and a mirrored aisle. It is not available in venues with ceiling heights below four meters.
