Royal Events Agency Cancellation Policy

The planning retainer is non-refundable once the kickoff meeting is completed.

If a client cancels more than one hundred twenty days before the event, Royal Events refunds 70 percent of planning fees paid beyond the retainer.

If a client cancels between one hundred nineteen and forty-five days before the event, Royal Events refunds 25 percent of planning fees paid beyond the retainer.

Cancellations inside forty-five days are not refundable because vendor coordination, production planning, and staff scheduling are already committed.

Royal Events allows one postponement within twelve months when requested at least sixty days before the event.
