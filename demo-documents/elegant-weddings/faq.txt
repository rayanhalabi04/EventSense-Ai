Elegant Weddings FAQ

Final guest count is due fourteen days before the wedding. The final invoice is adjusted using that confirmed guest count.

Outdoor sparkler exits are allowed only in the garden path after cake service and must be supervised by the venue coordinator.

Candles are allowed if they are enclosed in glass holders. Open flame candles, loose taper candles, and hanging flame decor are not allowed.

The venue includes two getting-ready rooms from 10:00 AM on the wedding day. Early access can be added when no morning event is scheduled.
