Elegant Weddings Pricing Packages

The Classic Garden Package starts at 4,800 USD for up to 120 guests. It includes ceremony seating, garden lighting, a bridal suite, day-of coordination, and basic floral styling.

The Pearl Ballroom Package starts at 7,200 USD for up to 180 guests. It includes ballroom rental, premium table styling, a five-hour event window, sound coordination, and a dedicated event captain.

The Signature Weekend Package starts at 11,500 USD for couples who want rehearsal support, wedding day coordination, and a farewell brunch setup. Catering is quoted separately.

Elegant Weddings requires a 20 percent deposit to reserve a date. Final package balance is due fourteen days before the wedding.
